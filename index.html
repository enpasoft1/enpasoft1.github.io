Meet Ernest: EnPaSoft Developer

Ernest Ansah is a software developer with expertise in computer hardware, computer architecture, and software development. He is a Teacher by profession,who was trained in the Presbyterian Training College. He earned his degree in General Science from the University of Education, Winneba, with Physics as one of his electives.

Furthering his education, Ernest pursued a degree in Computer Networking and Administration from the Presbyterian University in Ghana. He possesses an exceptional understanding of computer hardware and architecture, along with a commendable skill set in Microsoft Office, utilizing its diverse functionalities proficiently for various tasks. In addition to his degrees, he has a certificate in Python from Dive Into Code Japan.

He is also a social media Marketer and Social Media Content Creater

Beyond his technical prowess, Ernest has an extensive background in teaching, dedicating over fifteen years to educating students in multiple schools. His experience in teaching has honed his ability to simplify complex technical concepts and communicate effectively.

Ernest excels in group environments, leveraging his teaching background to collaborate effectively. His adaptability and willingness to work with diverse teams make him an invaluable asset in group projects.

Outside of work, Ernest enjoys exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and mentoring aspiring developers.
